This project develops innovative solutions for improving water quality and strengthening local economies across the Gulf of America watershed, complementing the NGIN Lab's low-cost environmental sensing work (see the [turbidity sensor project](/project/turbidity-sensor/)).

**Funding:** Environmental Protection Agency, Grant #982000 — *Innovative Solutions for Improving Water Quality and Strengthening Local Economies in the Gulf of America Watershed* ($993,953, 2025–)

**Team:** Susmit Shannigrahi (Co-PI)
