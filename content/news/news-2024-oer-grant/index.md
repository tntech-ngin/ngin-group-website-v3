The NGIN Lab has received an **Open Educational Resources (OER) Grant** from the Tennessee Board of Regents through the Tennessee Open Education initiative.

The grant supports the creation of a free and open-source textbook and lab materials for the undergraduate senior-level Computer Networks course (CSC 4200/5200), reducing the financial burden on students while improving course quality. Total amount: $17,381.

[Tennessee Open Education Program](https://www.tbr.edu/academics/tennessee-open-education)
