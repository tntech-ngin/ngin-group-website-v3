Our paper **"Looking for Errors TCP Misses"** has been accepted at IEEE NOMS 2025.

The paper investigates classes of transmission errors that TCP's checksum fails to detect in practice, quantifying the real-world risk to data integrity in large-scale file transfers. Authors: Jack Fitzgerald, Anju Gopinath, Logan Cadman, Sepideh Abdollah, Susmit Shannigrahi, and Craig Partridge.
