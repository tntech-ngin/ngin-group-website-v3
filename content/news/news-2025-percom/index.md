Our paper **"AquaCam: An ML-Enhanced Low-Cost, Deploy-Anywhere Water Level Detection Sensor"** has been accepted at IEEE PerCom 2025.

The work presents a low-cost, Raspberry Pi camera-based water level sensing system combining computer vision and edge ML to detect flood levels in real time without expensive instrumentation. Authors: Jacob Latham, Grace Dadzie, Alfred Kalyanapu, and Susmit Shannigrahi.
