Dr. Shannigrahi served as TPC Co-Chair for the **11th International Workshop on Computer and Networking Experimental Research using Testbeds (CNERT 2024)**, co-located with IEEE INFOCOM 2024 in Vancouver, Canada.

CNERT brings together researchers working on experimental networking infrastructure, testbeds, and reproducible network research. [Workshop details](https://infocom2024.ieee-infocom.org/11th-international-workshop-computer-and-networking-experimental-research-using-testbeds-call-papers)
